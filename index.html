Chapter IBorn Into War

June 9th. The Third Great Ninja War is burning through the land when, in the Uchiha clan, a boy is born.

He is four years old when his father shows him a battlefield.

The corpses. The ash. The cost.

Something in the child breaks that day — and something is decided. He will spend his whole life ending war.
Whatever it costs him.

Chapter IIIShisui

Shisui Uchiha. His best friend. The brother he chose for himself.

Together they dream of a village where clan and Leaf are one — and swear to protect it without bloodshed.

But Danzō does not believe in dreams. To use Shisui's power for himself, he steals one of his eyes.

Entrusting his last eye to Itachi, Shisui steps backward off the cliff — into the Naka River.

That night, grief carves Itachi's eyes into something new:
the Mangekyō Sharingan.

Chapter VThe Night

One night. One order. One choice that can never be taken back.

He asks to bear it alone — so no other hand is stained.

His parents do not run. They tell him they are proud.
They call him a kind child.

Only Sasuke is left.

For Sasuke to be spared, the elders' story must hold. So Itachi becomes the monster his brother needs to hate.

“Hate me. Detest me. Survive.”

He walks out of the village he just saved — a criminal in its history books,
crying where no one can see.

Chapter VIRed Clouds

He joins the Akatsuki — an organization of S-rank criminals — to watch it from within.

Beside the swordsman Kisame, he wears the red clouds of a wanted man.

To the world he is the clan-killer. In truth he is still Konoha's agent — and still Sasuke's shield. One quiet warning is all it takes to keep Danzō's hands off his brother:

“Touch him, and everything I know goes to every enemy you have.”

Chapter VIIThe Return

Three years later he walks back through the Leaf's gates — officially, “hunting the Nine-Tails.”

He breaks the famous Copy Ninja, Kakashi, with three seconds of Tsukuyomi — then leaves without taking anything at all.

The real message is for the elders:
I am alive. I remember. Keep your promise.

Chapter VIIIBrothers' War

Sasuke comes for him at last — raised on hatred, trained for exactly one purpose.

Amaterasu. Susanoo. A dragon of lightning called down from the sky.

What Sasuke doesn't know: his brother is already dying — nearly blind, riddled with illness, on his feet by will alone.

And every single move is a gift disguised as war — burning Orochimaru out of Sasuke's body, sealing away the curse, emptying a heart that never once held hatred for him.

“Forgive me, Sasuke. ……There won't be a next time.”

He dies smiling.

Chapter IXThe Truth

Then Obito tells Sasuke everything — and the world turns inside out.

The world called it a massacre.
It was a shield — carried for eight years, alone.

Chapter XReanimation

The Fourth Great War. A forbidden jutsu drags legends out of the grave — Itachi among them, a puppet on Kabuto's strings.

But years ago he hid a failsafe inside a crow: Shisui's eye, keyed to one command — protect the Leaf. It cuts his strings.

For one impossible day, the brothers fight side by side, and Izanami closes like a trap around the war's puppeteer.

“I am Itachi Uchiha, of the Hidden Leaf.”

Chapter XIFarewell

His borrowed time ends. Before the light takes him, he shows Sasuke everything — the whole truth, with no more lies between them.

You don't have to forgive me. But no matter what you do from now on — I will love you forever.

EpilogueThe Shadow of the Leaf

No statue stands for him. No page in the ledger of heroes.

He chose to be the villain of the story — so that the story could go on.

The truest shinobi guard the peace
from inside the shadows.
